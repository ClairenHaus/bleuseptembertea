Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for smaller gatherings and 4–6 weeks for larger events. Last-minute requests are welcome but subject to availability.
What areas do you serve?
We're based in Coolidge, Arizona and serve the greater Phoenix metro area including Chandler, Gilbert, Mesa, Tempe, Scottsdale, and surrounding communities. Travel fees may apply for events outside a 50-mile radius.
Is there a minimum guest count?
Our Intimate Tea Party package starts at just 2 guests. There's no minimum — we love small, intentional gatherings just as much as large celebrations.
Do you provide cups and servingware?
Yes! We offer both ceramic and disposable options depending on the package and your preference. Premium ceramic service is included in the Tea Bar package.
Can I customize the blends served?
Absolutely. We'll work with you to select blends that match your theme, season, and guest preferences. Custom labels and branding are also available for an additional fee.
What's the deposit and cancellation policy?
A 50% non-refundable deposit secures your date. The remaining balance is due 48 hours before the event. Cancellations made 7+ days in advance receive a credit toward a future booking.
