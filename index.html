The Story

Tea as ritual,
not routine.

Bleu September is an invitation to slow down. Rooted in ritual and crafted with intention, each blend is designed to complement life's quieter moments — the early morning hush, the gentle pause between tasks, the calm that settles as evening arrives.

Every tea is handmade from scratch — blended, labeled, and packaged by hand in Coolidge, Arizona. No shortcuts. No filler. Just tea made with love and an obsessive attention to what goes into your cup.

'Cause baby, it's Tea Time.
